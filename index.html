Sleep Device?

The device will go to deep sleep.

It will wake automatically when the RV starts moving.

ℹ️ HOW IT WORKS

Signal Strength

● ● ● ● all green Excellent
● ● ● ○ 3 green Good
● ● ○ ○ 2 yellow Weak
● ○ ○ ○ 1 red Very weak
○ ○ ○ ○ all grey No data

Circles appear in the header when connected. Based on how frequently data is arriving — updates every notification packet.

Sleep Timers

Phone disconnects Sleep in 10 min
SLEEP button pressed Sleep in 4 days
Motion — no phone Sleep in 20 min
Motion — after phone Sleep in 10 min

Wake Sources

Timer — normal Every 30 min
Timer — after SLEEP btn After 4 days
Physical button Instant — any time

While Sleeping

Every 30 minutes the device wakes briefly, checks if the RV has moved more than 2°, then goes back to sleep if parked. If movement is detected it stays awake and advertises via BLE so your phone can connect.

While Awake — No Phone

Checks angles every 1 minute. If the RV is still moving the sleep timer keeps resetting — the device stays awake for the entire drive. Once parked and angles stabilize the 20 minute countdown begins.

Power Use

Phone connected ~190mA
Awake — no phone ~2mA
Deep sleep ~0.02mA

Troubleshooting

If the device does not appear when scanning press the physical button in the upper cupboard beside the radio on the passenger side to wake it immediately.

If the app shows connected but no data is updating tap Disconnect then Connect again.
